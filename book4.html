The Catcher in the Rye

⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐ (5/5)

Author: J.D. Salinger

Genre: Coming-of-Age, Literary Fiction, Psychological Fiction

Description:

The Catcher in the Rye follows Holden Caulfield, a disenchanted teenager navigating life in New York City after being expelled from prep school. Through his cynical narration, the novel explores themes of teenage angst, alienation, and the struggle to find meaning and identity in a seemingly superficial world.
