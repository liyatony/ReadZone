Genre: Fantasy, Young Adult, Adventure

Description:

A young girl named Luna is accidentally fed moonlight instead of starlight and gains magical powers. Raised by a kind witch, a swamp monster, and a tiny dragon, she grows up unaware of her true abilities. As her magic emerges, she must face hidden truths and protect her loved ones.
