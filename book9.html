The Midnight Library

⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐ (5/5)

Author: Matt Haig

Genre: Philosophical Fiction, Fantasy

Description:

The Midnight Library follows Nora Seed, who finds herself in a mysterious library between life and death. Each book offers a chance to live a different version of her life based on the choices she could have made. The novel explores regret, possibility, and the value of living.
