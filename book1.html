The Alchemist

⭐⭐⭐⭐☆ (4/5)

Author: Paulo Coelho

Genre: Adventure, Fiction

Description

"The Alchemist" by Paulo Coelho is a philosophical novel about a young shepherd named Santiago who sets out on a journey to find a hidden treasure. Along the way, he meets mentors, faces challenges, and discovers the importance of following one's dreams. The story inspires readers to listen to their hearts and pursue their true purpose.
