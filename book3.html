Pride and Prejudice

⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐ (5/5)

Author: Jane Austen

Genre: Romance, Historical Fiction, Social Commentary

Description:

Pride and Prejudice is a classic novel by Jane Austen that explores themes of love, class, and social expectations in 19th-century England. The story follows the strong-willed Elizabeth Bennet as she navigates family pressures and her evolving relationship with the proud Mr. Darcy. With wit and sharp commentary, Austen delivers a timeless tale of personal growth and societal critique.
